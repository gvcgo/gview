This document assists gview users and developers with sharing issues,
suggestions and patches.

# Issues and suggestions

Please include as much relevant information as possible.

Review project [documentation](https://pkg.go.dev/github.com/gvcgo/gview)
before submitting an issue.

Share issues and suggestions on the [issue tracker](https://github.com/gvcgo/gview/issues).

# Patches (pull requests)

Submit patches as [pull requests](https://github.com/gvcgo/gview/pulls).
